About Us

From standard foldable large containers (FLCs), to simple Injection Moulded Bins Conpack designs customized packaging solutions which can compatible to pack small nut bolt to huge panels and Linkages of any automotive vehicles.

Conpack also offer composite packaging solutions to various customers based on the product profile and it is sensitiveness in handling and assembly to the vehicles. Materials used include polypropylene (PP), polyethylene (PE) and acrylonitrile butadiene styrene (ABS) in different densities.

The main ingredient chosen as plastic because of Light weight, flexible and compatible to take a load close to 1 MT and further any packaging solution with PP is easy to clean and maintain.

Conpack has a team of Design Engineers trained in Germany to provide customized solutions. Their core activity is to discuss, assimilate, design & deliver products that not only fits the desired requirement of customer but also to the packaging budget.

Rest assured any customer can rely on Conpack Pooling India right from conceptualization, Design, Proto Typing and Implemention of Packaigng on Turn Key basis with Latest packaging technology at par with Global Standards.

Their exclusive ware houses and trained technocrats across india with the back up of software make the things lean and informative.

Conpack Group

With over 40+ years of collective experience, people @ Conpack bring a wide array of expertise to its solutions table. The conpack group has been a pioneer in the field of Packaging solutions from a long time

  • 2002 - Concept Pack Company Formed
  • 2003 - Added Corrugation Unit and Converting equipment to Convert Premium Corrugated fiber containers of NF,BF ,AAA Paper Boards.
  • 2004 - Added Advanced Wood Working Machines to focus more on Processed Wood such as plywood (commercial and marine grade) Mfg and Applications.
  • 2005 - ISO and D&B Certified Accredition and growth accelerated at the rate of 100% by YOY
  • 2009 - Added imported Converting Equipment to Produce Export worthy Higher GSM Premium Paper Board.
  • 2011 - Corrugation Capacity expands to meet the export demand.
  • 2011 - Widened the Customer base and Geographical spread of activities (Chennai unit).
  • 2012 - Formed Separate Vertical to Import Paper from USA and Engineered Wood OSB and QSB from Europe.
  • 2012 - Enhanced Value Added works such as Foam, wooden cabinets, metal cabinets, work stations and lab tables etc.,.
  • 2013 - Got into new technology of REUSABLE PACKAGING.
