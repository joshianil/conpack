Conpack Foldable Large Containers (FLC)

Conpack range of foldable large containers brings the magnamity of the bulk containers with the ease of use of foldable containers. Be it any industry our products are equipped to cater to your demands and become an integral part of your process.


Our range of light-duty, medium-duty and heavy-duty designs are manufactured keeping in mind the industry requirements and to meet a wide range of application requirements. Our containers meet today's demanding requirement of material handling and distribution systems.


Features and Benefits

  • Ensure superior product protection with robust design features including ergonomic latches & handles, wear & damage resistant material and modular construction
  • Increases transport and storage space efficiency – containers are stackable when full or folded
  • Ability to access bulk parts line side through the use of the side access doors
  • Streamline line side processes with single person operation and the elimination of strapping and wrapping material
  • Improve handling flexibility and product security with four-way forklift access
  • Reduce time and effort for opening/closing access doors and collapsing containers with large, spring-loaded ergonomic latches
  • Eliminate packaging waste and the associated time and cost with fully recyclable materials
  • Reduces costs along the entire supply chain using a single container from Tier Supplier to line side
