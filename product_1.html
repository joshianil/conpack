Conpack Standard Pallet, LID & Sleeve Systems (PLS)

Best solution for maximizing space. Conpack specializes in the corrugated plastic based PLS systems. These sleeves "z-fold" to collapse flat for easier shipping and storage and then pop open to slide into grooves designed into the pallet base and cap creating a sturdy shipping container.

These pallets are consistent in size, shape, and weight. The primary advantage of this type of pallet is that its reusable and depending on how you use it, it can last for atleast 250 trips.

Conpack not only makes standard pallets, it can customize the pallets as per your needs. We are known for our customization skills and can matchup to you exact requirements.

Technical Specifications

  • Anti-slide inserts for pallet runners
  • Customized color (min. order of 3000 pcs)
  • Printing on the container wall-ring (1 color)
  • Customized wall-ring height

Sleeve benefits

  • Up to 30% larger usable interior volume
  • Collapsible, providing up to 10:1 return ratio
  • Up to 90% reduction in transport costs
  • 100% recyclable
  • Easy to set up
  • Customizable to different weights and sizes
  • Durable for an exceedingly long life
  • Resistant to tearing, impact and compression
